ASL Translator tool

Our mission is to asssist communication of individuals.

Core of our project

The ASL Translator is an advanced technological tool that leverages machine learning and computer vision techniques by using a camera to detect gestures including movements of hands, head, and facial expressions, and translating them into a text in real-time. It is designed to facilitate communication for individuals who are hard of hearing with others who are not familiar with sign language.

Used data

We are utilizing the Microsoft ASL dataset which comprises three JSON files contaiting training, validation, and test sets. Each dataset includes a comprehensive list of classes representing words to be identified. Each data point comes with a URL to a video of a person performing the corresponding sign along with video length, start and end time, bounding boxes, etc.

User tips

Due to limitation of current model, the translation process can be a bit rough at times.
For best results, make sure that:
- You're facing the camera head on
- Your entire face, arms, and hands are visible in frame
- The scene is well lit
- Nobody else is in the frame with you
